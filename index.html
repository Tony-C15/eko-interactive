About




EKO interactive aims to provide a customizable environment where teachers can create fun and engaging games for their students with no coding knowledge required. Modern classrooms have an abundance of technology but are not using them to their full potential. There are currently no games designed with a curriculum in mind. So this makes it more difficult for teachers to create a fun and engaging learning environment for their students. That is the problem that EKO interactive wants to solve.
